Fluffy pancakes

HIGH PROTEIN MEAL PREP FLUFFY PANCAKES.

(220 Cals, 22g Protein, 9g Fat, 12g Carbs per meal).

Store in the fridge up to 3 days or Keep it frozen in the freezer & reheat using a toaster.

The ingredients used in this is to make one big meal prep to then portion equally into 5 servings‼


Ingredients

  1. 6 large eggs
  2. 90g Protein powder/flavour of your choice
  3. Teaspoon of baking powder
  4. 200g Banana

Steps

  1. Blend all your ingredients together.
  2. Add equal portions of batter onto the pan and cook for 2-3 mins on medium heat‼
  3. Should make roughly 15 Pancakes.
  4. Portion the servings equally based on how many pancakes you make‼
  5. Use baking paper to separate pancakes so its easy to compartmentalise when storing in the freezer or fridge.
  6. Add optional low cal chocolate sauce when reheated or toppings of choice‼
